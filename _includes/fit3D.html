An optimal transport map between $U[0,1]^3$ and a trivariate Gaussian sample of size $10$ (blue points). The cube (representing the support of $U[0,1]^3$) is partitioned into $10$ convex polyhedra, where every points within each polyhedron are transported to the corresponding sample point. The orange points are the centroids of the polyhedra and indicates the correspondence between sample points and the partitions.
